# Example concept: Data minimization

> **Example page (concept).** Shows a concept page following the depth standard, linking to norm nodes and landmark decisions — this is how the backlinks emerge that replace the graph query.

## Definition / Overview

The principle of data minimization requires that personal data be processed in a manner that is adequate, relevant and limited to what is necessary for the purpose. It is one of the foundational processing principles and pervades every lawfulness assessment.

## Core aspects

### Relationship to the legal basis
Even processing that is permissible under [[DSGVO Art. 6]] must additionally satisfy the minimization requirement — permission and minimization are cumulative.

### Temporal dimension
Data minimization also operates temporally: indefinite retention is regularly disproportionate.

## References & controversies

Scope of the minimization requirement for advertising-financed services; relationship to purpose limitation.
